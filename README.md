# iLovePDF – Free Online PDF Tools

[![Website](https://img.shields.io/badge/Website-iLovePDF-blue)](https://i-lovepdf.site)

iLovePDF is a free **online PDF tools** platform that lets you **merge, split, compress, convert, edit, and optimize PDF files** quickly, securely, and without any installation. Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone who needs fast PDF management online.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Merge PDF** – Combine multiple PDF files into one single document.
- **Split PDF** – Extract specific pages or split large PDFs into smaller files.
- **Compress PDF** – Reduce PDF file size without losing quality.
- **Convert PDF** – Convert PDFs to Word, Excel, PNG, JPG, and vice versa.
- **Edit PDF** – Edit text, add annotations, sign, or modify your PDF online.
- **Optimize PDF** – Unlock PDFs, protect them with passwords, rotate, reorder, and extract pages.
